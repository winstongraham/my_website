My Journey

I’m an aspiring data engineer with a strong foundation in Physics, data analysis, and programming. After completing my BSc in Physics from the University of Exeter, I've applied my analytical skills to various projects, ranging from data analysis with Python to STEM tutoring.

As I transition into the field of data engineering, I’m excited to begin the Generation UK Data Engineering bootcamp, which will provide me with the advanced technical skills needed to become proficient in managing and optimizing data pipelines, working with large datasets, and leveraging cloud-based infrastructure.

My ultimate goal is to contribute to data-driven decision-making processes and build scalable data systems that empower organizations to derive actionable insights.
